🔹 About CrackSmart Academy

At CrackSmart Academy, we believe that every student deserves the right guidance, the right skills, and the right opportunities to succeed. Founded by Preethi Reddy, CrackSmart was born from a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations.

We understand the challenges faced by students when stepping into the real world — especially when it comes to placements, interviews, and building confidence. That’s why our programs deliver end-to-end career readiness with a personal touch.

From mastering programming languages like C, C++, Java, and Python, to building expertise in Data Structures & Algorithms, SQL, and Aptitude, we ensure every learner builds a rock-solid foundation. What makes us stand out is our hands-on learning – with real-time projects in Web Development and Artificial Intelligence, students gain practical experience that sets them apart in interviews.

  • ✅ Resume building & LinkedIn optimization
  • ✅ Mock interviews and group discussion training
  • ✅ One-on-one mentorship & personalized feedback
